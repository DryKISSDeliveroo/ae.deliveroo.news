Deliveroo and the Jameel Arts Centre team up to deliver 300 restaurants to art lovers in Dubai

  • 1 March 2019
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 2 min
food art
Share this article

Enjoy a picnic and specialty chocolate bar in a picturesque setting at Jameel Arts Centre

In celebration of Dubai Art Month, Deliveroo is teaming up with Jameel Arts Centre, the newest addition to the cultural landscape and is the first contemporary arts museum in Dubai. Throughout the month of March, art lovers in the Centre craving more than just exhibitions are in for a treat, as they now have access to over 300 restaurants on Deliveroo.

After ordering their favourite meals through the app direct to the Centre, customers in Jameel Arts Centre need to head to the Deliveroo pick up point, where they will receive their order in a wicker picnic hamper, which is a piece of art in itself.  Gallery-goers can then dive into their delicious meal on the grass area overlooking the Jaddaf Waterfront Sculpture Park and the Creek.

Inside this wicker picnic basket customers will find a kit complete with a rollout mat, condiments and a set of Jameel Arts Centre's Artist's Rooms books to read in the park. Order from artistic, hearty brands like Sushi Art, Cali-Poke and Nu-TRAY-tious along with the hundreds of other options that deliver to the Jameel Arts Centre location.

Every customer who orders to Jameel Art Centre will also receive a Mirzam chocolate bar, on the house. Designed by talented UAE artist, Khalid Mezaina, the chocolate delivers on both flavour, with ingredients familiar to the region including rose water and pistachios, and the artistically designed packaging - the perfect memento from a day out at Jameel Arts Centre.
