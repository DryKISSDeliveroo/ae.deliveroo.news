Deliveroo celebrates art month with paintable cookies

  • 7 March 2018
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 1 min
art cookies
Share this article

It's art month in Dubai and Deliveroo is hopping on board. For the 7th edition of Art Week taking place between March 17th-24th, the UAE's beloved food delivery service has teamed up with SugarMoo, the homegrown e-bakery, to offer users the chance to put on their artist hats and paint their own Roo-shaped cookies.

With every order from these partners from the Editions Kitchen -- PINZA!, Burger & Lobster, Fraîche, Clinton St.Baking Co., Editions Greatest Hits, Barsalata, Hippy Deli & Hen House – a complimentary a pack of cookies will be included with:

  • 1 edible paint palette
  • 1 Roo-shaped cookie
  • 1 paintbrush to decorate

It's time to get creative and show Dubai what you've got!  Upload your cookie creations onto social media with the hashtag #Deliverooartweek

Life is short, paint the cookie and eat it.

*This promo will run from 17th-24th March.
